Fair Spins. Fast Decisions.

RandomWheel is a free, high-performance web utility designed to make decision-making fun and unbiased.

Our Mission

We aim to provide the most responsive and customizable random picker tool on the web. Whether you are a teacher picking students or a group of friends deciding on dinner, we make the process instant and visual.

Privacy First

Unlike other tools, we don't store your lists on a database. Everything happens in your browser's memory, ensuring your private data stays exactly where it belongs: with you.
